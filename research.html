Research

ETOS – Research positioning

ETOS is positioned at the intersection of decision theory, ethics, governance and human–AI interaction.

Back to international Contact
Status

A conceptual version of the framework has been submitted to SCAI 2026. The project remains open for interdisciplinary collaboration and critical development.

Decision theory

Beyond single-rule optimisation

ETOS examines situations in which choices cannot be reduced to one stable optimal rule.

Ethics

Normative tension made visible

The framework focuses on the relationship between stated values, actual action and lived consequence.

Governance and AI

Proxy metrics, legitimacy and consequence

ETOS offers a structured language for analysing where systems begin to lose coherence.

Open collaboration

Interdisciplinary development is welcome

ETOS is open to research dialogue, critical feedback, collaborative refinement and pilot-oriented conceptual work.

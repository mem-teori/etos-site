Start here

ETOS is a place to begin when things do not hold together.

It is built for situations where something may look correct on paper, while lived experience tells a different story.

What?
A framework for observation, reflection and learning.
Why?
To make responsibility and consequences visible.
How?
First understanding, then depth, then use.

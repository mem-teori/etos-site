🏠 ETOS Omtanke · Ready for pilot

You may not lock the door.
But you can understand the situation.

Care homes may not restrict residents with dementia — but staff lack the tools to assess the situation. ETOS Omtanke provides an informed assessment instead of a reactive alarm: context, risk and action recommendations in 3 seconds.

Citizen entry

When systems look right but feel wrong.

ETOS makes visible the tensions between what was promised, what was done, what actually happened, and what people experienced afterwards.

What many recognise
  • Improvements are promised, but daily life does not feel better.
  • The numbers move in the right direction, but trust falls.
  • Responsibility becomes so distributed that it disappears.

Background

Why does this universe exist?

Mærke

Children can sense things

Long before they have words for it. This universe gives them a language — not to explain it away, but to stay with it.

Forstå

Many learn to ignore it

Adults often tell them that it probably is not that serious. But what they feel is real. It is worth listening to.

Handle

A foundation for life

Children who learn to listen to themselves are better equipped for boundaries, relationships, and finding their own voice.

Method

Somatic attention

The universe builds on research into bodily awareness and early emotional regulation — presented in an accessible format.

Progression

Age-adapted progression

The three tracks are designed to build on one another — from sensing and safety to self-understanding and action.

Collaboration

Developed in dialogue

The project is being developed with a focus on real experiences from children and families — and in dialogue with organisations such as Mødrehjælpen.

Background

Why does this universe exist?

Sense

Children can sense things

Long before they have words for it. This universe gives them a language — not to explain it, but to stay in it.

Understand

Many learn to ignore it

We adults often tell children it's probably not that bad. But what they sense is real. It's worth listening to.

Act

A foundation for life

Children who learn to listen to themselves are better equipped for boundaries, relationships, and finding their own voice.

Method

Somatic awareness

The universe is built on research into bodily awareness and early emotional regulation — presented in an accessible format.

Progression

Age-appropriate track

The three tracks are designed to build on each other — from sensing and safety to self-understanding and action.

Collaboration

Developed in dialogue

The project is developed with a focus on real experiences from children and families — and in dialogue with organisations such as Mødrehjælpen.

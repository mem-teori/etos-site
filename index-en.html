The essentials first

In 5 seconds

What ETOS is, where it can be used, and why it matters now.

What is ETOS?

A framework for seeing what usually gets lost.

ETOS makes visible the points where intention, system and human experience no longer align.

Where is it used?

Where decisions have real human consequences.

Psychiatry, democracy, AI governance, public administration and other fields where decisions are felt in reality.

Why now?

Because systems are getting stronger — while learning lags behind.

More decisions are made through rules, procedures and technology, while responsibility and consequence often fall between layers.

M.E.M. as a core idea

One theory — many applications

M.E.M. is not designed as a closed concept, but as a way of understanding situations. That is why the same foundation can be used in very different universes — from children’s emotional lives to difficult decisions in systems.

That is also why the front page does not present only one product. It presents the places where the theory becomes alive in different ways.

Gå til M.E.M. → Read more about the idea

M

Meaning — what is experienced as important, desired, or threatened in the situation.

E

Experience — lived reality, relationship, and what is felt before it is explained.

M

Model — the structures, rules, and forms that organise action and understanding.

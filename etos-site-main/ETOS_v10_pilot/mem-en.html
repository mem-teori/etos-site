M.E.M.

Meaning – Experience – Model

M.E.M. is the theoretical framework behind ETOS. It describes how values, lived human experience and systemic models generate normative tensions.

Core

Meaning concerns what is said, promised and intended. Experience concerns lived reality and consequence. Model concerns rules, metrics, structures and system logic.

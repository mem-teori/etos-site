# ETOS go-live checklist

## 1. Google Search Console
- Open Google Search Console.
- Add `https://mem-teori.github.io/etos-site/` as a property.
- Verify ownership.
- Submit `https://mem-teori.github.io/etos-site/sitemap.xml`.
- Request indexing for:
  - `index.html`
  - `om.html`
  - `anvendelse.html`
  - `presse.html`
  - `samarbejde.html`

## 2. Quick launch flow
When you share the site publicly, use this order:
1. `index.html`
2. `om.html`
3. then one of these depending on audience:
   - `presse.html`
   - `samarbejde.html`
   - `kontakt.html`

## 3. Good sharing targets
- Press and media contacts
- Professional organisations
- Researchers or academic contacts
- Potential pilot partners
- Relevant LinkedIn posts or professional networks

## 4. Keep the front door simple
Send people to the front page or Start here first.
Avoid sending first-time visitors straight to the deep theory pages.

## 5. Optional next step
Add privacy-friendly analytics later if you want visitor data.

The problem

When learning starts the wrong way round

Many children encounter science through explanations, concepts, and demands — before they've had a chance to experience it.

For some children, that creates curiosity. For others, it creates distance.

Especially in special education, we see that learning often becomes too verbal and abstract — losing the children before they've even started.

Today

Explanation → Understanding → Experience

Learning-uniVRs

Experience → Understanding → Explanation

The consequence

Children who never get to feel it learn that science is for other people.

The solution

Experience before explanation

Learning-uniVRs is an experimental learning environment where children first get to explore — and understand afterwards.

🤲

🔬

The experience

What it feels like

01

The child steps in

A calm universe opens up. There are no tasks. No requirements. Only things to explore. The child decides for themselves what's interesting.

02

Something catches their attention

An element lights up. A reaction happens. The child moves closer — not because they have to, but because they want to. Curiosity is already awake.

03

The experiment begins

The child combines, changes, tries things. Something works. Something doesn't. Both are learning. There are no wrong answers here.

04

Understanding emerges

Afterwards — once the experience is in the body — the explanation makes sense. Not as abstract theory, but as something the child already knows.

Pilot project · own platform

First step: Science Sandbox

The first version is a browser-based, animated learning world — built from the ground up on its own foundation, not on top of a licensed game. Element cores, reaction fields, and energy zones instead of blocks and crafting grids, so the concept is entirely your own from the start.

⚛️ Build molecules

Gather atoms in the Atom Workshop and see what emerges. Water, rust, carbon — built with your own hands.

🌡️ Change the temperature

What happens to water when it gets hot or cold? Experience phase changes directly in the Temperature Zone.

🔭 Experiment freely

No right answers. Only discoveries. Mistakes are part of the process — and it's okay to try again.

🗺️ Four missions

Central Plaza, Atom Workshop, Reaction Station, and more zones await — each with its own goal.

Foundation

Built on M.E.M.

Learning-uniVRs grows out of M.E.M. — Meaning, Experience, Model. An approach where understanding arises through experience, not the other way around. It is not a teaching method. It is a principle about how people actually learn.

M

Meaning

Meaning is created when something resonates from within.

E

Experience

Experience is the starting point — not the goal.

M

Model

The model emerges afterwards — as a frame for what was experienced.

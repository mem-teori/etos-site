Professional entry

ETOS is a reflective decision architecture for systems where legitimate considerations cannot always be reconciled.

ETOS makes divergence between intention, action, effect and lived reality visible, connecting analysis with reflection, standing debt and learning over time.

Keywords
  • Divergence as structural signal
  • Standing debt as accumulated consequence
  • Learning loops instead of pure evaluation

What you know

Du prøver at hjælpe.
Det virker ikke.
Du mister dig selv.

Carers of people with addiction find themselves in one of the most complex tension fields that exists. There is no right solution — only choices that all come with a cost.

ETOS is not here to give you the answer. It is here to make the tension visible — so you can act with more clarity.

Du prøver at hjælpe — men hjælpen giver ikke resultat. Det føles som om du gør alt forkert.

Du føler ansvar — men kan ikke afgøre hvad der er dit ansvar og hvad der ikke er.

Du elsker vedkommende — men du er ved at miste dig selv i processen.

Du ved ikke hvornår du skal holde fast, hvornår du skal give slip — og hvornår du skal gå.

The programme

Four modules · four steps

Click a module to see its content and exercise.

01

Understand the situation

What is actually happening?
Content
Introduction to ETOS — simple and concrete
Helping vs. protecting yourself
Love vs. boundaries
Hope vs. reality
Exercise
👉 "Your situation"
You adjust three sliders: risk, relational trust and your own burden. ETOS shows your current tension field.
Clarity over situationen Named tension
02

See yourself

Where do I stand?
Content
Responsibility vs. guilt — the important difference
What is mine — what is not mine
Ethical debt: what you owe yourself
Exercise
👉 "My responsibility"
Three columns: What I can influence · What I cannot · What I think I should be able to. The separation is liberating.
Clarity Relief Realism
03

Beslutninger

What do I do now?
Content
ETOS as a decision tool in practice
When to act — and when to stop
Setting boundaries without giving up the relationship
Exercise
👉 "Next steps"
What is one action? What is one boundary? What is one thing you stop? Three concrete answers — not plans, but steps.
Concrete action A boundary set
04

Relationship and future

How do I live with this?
Content
Relationship without control — what it requires
Acceptance without giving up
Rebuilding your own life alongside
Exercise
👉 "My life alongside"
What gives me energy? What have I lost? What do I want to rebuild? Three answers that point forward — not back.
Own life visible Direction forward
Optional element

Bankeren in the programme

For carers who know Bankeren from the children's universe — or who simply need to feel something before talking about it — Bankeren is a quiet tool in the programme.

Ikke obligatorisk. But stærkt i de samtaler hvor ord ikke er nok til at starte.

See the Bankeren product →
Example from a session
Participant: "I don't know what I feel. It's just... heavy."
→ Chooses purple colour on Bankeren
"Purple. That fits. It is something inward."
The conversation starts from there — from what was felt, not what was explained.
Format

Three ways to participate

🏛

Workshop

All four modules in one day. Intensive and concrete. Suitable for groups who know each other.

3–4 hours · in person · small groups
📅

Programme

One module per week over four weeks. Time to let the insights land and work in everyday life.

4 weeks · online or in person · group or solo

1:1 counselling

ETOS as a conversation tool in individual sessions. Most flexible and personally adapted.

Arranged individually · online or in person

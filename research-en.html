What is being explored

Four central research tracks

The Simulation Lab is not only a demo collection. It is an attempt to build a readable environment for questions that otherwise often remain hidden behind black-box optimisation.

Recursive governance

Can a system learn under governance?

ETOS explores whether a system can correct itself without slipping outside its own constitutional constraints.

Drift

Can normative movement be detected early?

Drift, velocity, acceleration, and oscillation are used as practical signals for whether a system is moving away from its purpose.

Ethical debt

What happens to the consequences afterwards?

Not all decisions disappear once they have been made. ETOS treats ethical debt as something that accumulates and requires active resolution.

Alignment

Can a system say no to its own improvement?

The central alignment question is tested concretely: whether the system can reject a change that looks efficient, but quietly changes the ruler.

The problem

Systems are built for one kind of thinking

Most planning tools expect you to think linearly — one thing at a time, in order, broken into tasks.

For many people, that's the exact opposite of how thoughts actually work. Meaning comes before structure. Everything is connected. Overview emerges when something is allowed to exist raw.

That's not a problem with you. It's a problem with the system.

What the systems expect
  • Think linearly
  • Prioritise before you understand
  • Break everything into tasks
  • Have it under control from the start
What actually happens
  • Meaning comes before structure
  • Everything is connected at once
  • Overview emerges when something exists raw
  • Movement creates clarity

Klassifikation

Five states

ETOS classifies every situation into one of five states — from normal to constitutional veto.

Normal
The decision is in balance — act with confidence
Requires review
Tensions are present — conscious reflection required
Emergency
High tension — ethical debt registered
Incompatible
The system cannot fulfil its own goals
Constitutional veto
Fundamental principles are broken — requires human intervention

"ETOS does not make decisions — it makes them visible."

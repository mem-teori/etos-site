ETOS / M.E.M.

Model first. Heavy explanation later.

M.E.M. is the theoretical frame. ETOS is the way that frame becomes usable in analysis, reflection and systemic learning.

Intention

The intended purpose, official rationale or normative promise.

System

The rules, structures and procedures shaping the situation.

Action

What was actually done in practice.

Experience

How the consequences were lived and understood.

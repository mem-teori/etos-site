Real example

How ETOS applies in reality

Intention

A municipality wants to help a citizen through support and regulation.

System

The case is governed by rules, budgets and administrative procedures.

Action

A caseworker makes a decision based on guidelines and available options.

Experience

The citizen experiences loss of control, misunderstanding or lack of support.

ETOS identifies the gap between intention, system, action and lived experience — making the tension visible.

What ETOS does

From observation to learning

1. Detect

Identifies when structural tension is present in a decision.

2. Classify

Analyzes the type and severity of the tension.

3. Learn

Feeds the outcome back into the system to improve future decisions.

**Label/Badge:** Læringsplatform · Under udvikling

LæringsUniVRs — Oplevelse før forklaring

**Brødtekst:**
Et nyt læringsrum hvor børn udforsker naturvidenskab gennem oplevelse — ikke forklaring.

Vi starter med kemi og fysik i et trygt og velkendt miljø: Minecraft.
Her kan elever bygge molekyler, ændre temperatur og se reaktioner ske i realtid —
*uden opgaver, uden krav, kun nysgerrighed.*

Næste trin er et fuldt immersivt VR-læringsrum i samarbejde med bl.a. Khora.

🧱 **Minecraft Science Sandbox** · Klar til pilot

🥽 **LæringsUniVRs · VR** · Under udvikling
Et fuldt immersivt rum hvor børn kan træde ind i molekyler, kemiske reaktioner og fysiske fænomener.

📐 **Alle naturfag** · Planlagt
Samme princip. Forskellige universer. Kemi → Fysik → Biologi → Matematik → Geografi.

**Label/Badge:** Learning platform · In development

Learning-uniVRs — Experience before explanation

**Body text:**
A new learning space where children explore science through experience — not explanation.

We start with chemistry and physics in a safe, familiar environment: Minecraft.
Here, students can build molecules, change temperature, and watch reactions happen in real time —
*no tasks, no requirements, only curiosity.*

The next step is a fully immersive VR learning space, developed in partnership with Khora and others.

**The three tracks:**

🧱 **Minecraft Science Sandbox** · Ready for pilot
Students build molecules, experiment with phase transitions, and explore chemistry freely.
A teacher track is included.

🥽 **Learning-uniVRs · VR** · In development
A fully immersive space where children can step inside molecules, chemical reactions, and physical phenomena.

📐 **All science subjects** · Planned
Same principle. Different universes. Chemistry → Physics → Biology → Mathematics → Geography.

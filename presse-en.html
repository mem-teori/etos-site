Press angle

When complex structural tensions need to be explained clearly

ETOS can be used to describe why decisions may look correct inside a system while being experienced very differently in reality.

That makes the model relevant in stories about psychiatry, democracy, AI governance, public administration and other fields where responsibility, learning and consequence become split apart.

Short angle: ETOS asks what happens when intention, system, action and lived reality no longer hold together.

The Banker grows up

The first thing you felt — and the thing that is starting to take shape.

What it's about

Small stories, big feelings

At this age, children start navigating social spaces. They sense a lot — but don't always have the words. This universe lets them recognise themselves.

Theme

Feelings without a name

Something hurts, but you don't know what. Something is funny, but you don't dare show it. The stories meet children where they are.

Theme

Friendships

Who is my friend? What do you do when you disagree? And what is it that knocks, when something isn't right?

Theme

Small conflicts

What feels big to a child, is big. The universe takes it seriously — without making it heavier than it is.

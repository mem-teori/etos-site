Use cases

Where ETOS can be used

ETOS is relevant wherever decisions and systems have real human consequences.

Psychiatry

When care, coercion and lived reality point in different directions.

ETOS helps make tensions visible between treatment, rules and patient experience.

Democracy

When institutions promise representation, but people experience distance.

ETOS helps read fractures between ideal, practice and trust.

AI governance

When automation requires responsibility, traceability and reflection.

ETOS can frame where technology, model and human consequence no longer fit together.

Public administration

When rules, budgets and practice create imbalance in reality.

ETOS helps gather consequences into learning rather than leaving them split across layers.
